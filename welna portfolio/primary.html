SK TAGAROH

Years Attended: Tabika KEMAS (2010) & Year 1–6 (2011–2016)

I began my early education at Tabika KEMAS SK Tagaroh before continuing my primary school years from Year 1 to Year 6 at SK Tagaroh between 2011 and 2016. During this time, I developed a strong foundation in academics and started exploring leadership through various co-curricular activities.

By Year 4 and Year 5, I became more actively involved in school activities and was entrusted with important roles such as:

  • President of Tunas Puteri Girl Scouts
  • Head of Athletics
  • Treasurer of the Crime Prevention Club

These roles helped build my confidence and taught me responsibility and teamwork at an early age. As for my academic performance, I recall achieving 2As in the UPSR examination.
